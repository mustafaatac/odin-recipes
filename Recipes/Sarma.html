Sarma

Description

Sarma, a beloved Turkish classic, features grape leaves wrapped around a flavorful filling of rice, herbs, and spices. With hints of dill, parsley, and lemon, it’s a perfect balance of zesty and savory flavors, enjoyed warm with yogurt or cold as a mezze.

What makes Sarma special is the care in its preparation, often made together with family. This timeless dish is more than food – it’s a symbol of Turkish hospitality and tradition, offering a taste of culture in every bite.

Ingredients

For the Filling

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 medium onions, finely chopped
  • ⅔ cup short-grain rice, rinsed
  • ½ bunch fresh parsley, finely chopped
  • ½ bunch fresh dill, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried mint
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt

For Assembly

  • 40 fresh or jarred grape leaves, rinsed and stems removed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
